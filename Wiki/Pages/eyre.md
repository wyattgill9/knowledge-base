# eyre

eyre is an alternative to [[anyhow]] for dynamic error handling in Rust, offering richer error reports with custom handlers. It's the better choice when you want formatted, human-readable error output with context chains — especially for CLIs and user-facing applications. See [[rust-error-handling]].
